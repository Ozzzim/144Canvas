Draw anything you want!

As long as it fits 256 by 144 canvas

This site allows you to create and publish pixelart pictures via in-browser tool. Enjoy!

Rules

1. Be nice to one another. If you feel like the drawing isn't all that good, offer advice

2. Do not steal others artwork. That includes all art in general, not just on-site pieces

3. Do not post generally offensive content. Especially if it's illegal in your (or any) country

4. Do not advertise anything.

5. Do not abuse the rules. Just because you found a loophole, doesn't mean you can use it
